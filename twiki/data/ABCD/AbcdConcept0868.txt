Gathering

---+++++ Full Name

End of gathering event (time of day)

---+++++ Documentation

The time of day in hours from midnight for the end of the gathering event.

---+++++ Content

The time of day in decimal hours from midnight local time for the end of the gathering event. 

Note that times are decimal hours: 13.5 represents 1:30 pm, not 1:05 pm, nor 1:50 pm. 

---++++ Recommended or Prescribed Values


---+++++ Example Value

13.5 

04.25
