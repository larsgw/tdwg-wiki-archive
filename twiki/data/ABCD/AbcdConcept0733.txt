IPEN number

---+++++ Content

A unique number given to plant materials accessioned in conformance with the rules of the International Plant Exchange Network (see http://www.bgci.org/abs/ipen_numbers.html).

---++++ Recommended or Prescribed Values


---+++++ Example Value
XX-0-WU-1989/2769

US-0-THARF-NA23476

GE-1-M-03/2855w

---+++++ Example Explanation
XX for unknown country of origin, 0 no special caveats for handing on the material, WU = Bot. Garden Vienna has introduced the material to the network and holds original infromation, 1989/2769: Accession number in WU.

Second example analoguous, origin is United States, introducing garden is Tharandt.

Third example: Material from Georgia, restricted (restrictions have to be enquired at the botanical garden M = Munich).
