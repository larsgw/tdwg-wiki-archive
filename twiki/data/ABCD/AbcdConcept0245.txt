The full name of the administrative contact for the unit record in a form appropriate for sorting alphabetically. (vCard: Sort-String) 

---+++++ Content

The full name of the administrative contact for the unit record in a form appropriate for sorting alphabetically. 

The form consists of the following: Family name first followed by a comma then a space then given names or initials separated by a space.
