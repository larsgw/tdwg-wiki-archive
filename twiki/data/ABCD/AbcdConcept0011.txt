---+++++ Content

An administrative contact usually representing the agent acting as the original supplier or custodian of the dataset. This is a GBIF UDDI registry element (Administrative-Contact). 

Questions and feedback about data, or restrictions on use of the data, should be directed to the administrative contact. 

If an administrative contact is given on the unit-level then that one should be used for end-user purposes.
