---+++++ Full Name

Date of specimen acquisition

---+++++ Documentation

A textual statement of the date of acquisition of the specimen by the institution.

---+++++ Content

The date that the specimen was acquired by the institution.

Date on which the accession entered the collection as YYYYMMDD. Missing data (MM or DD) should be indicated with hyphens. Leading zeros are required. A FAO/IPGRI multi-crop passport descriptor (MCPD), description and examples taken from EURISCO descriptor list dated July 3, 2002.
