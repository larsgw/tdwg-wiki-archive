The suffix for the name of the data collection owner.

---+++++ Content

The suffix for the name of the data collection owner. The suffix should be output after the name, regardless of whether it is in sorting sequence (Inherited, Given) or not. 

Examples: 'Jun.', 'III.'. (vCard: N-Suffix)
