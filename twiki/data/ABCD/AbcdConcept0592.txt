---+++++ Full Name

Period specimen held by previous collection is explicit

---+++++ Documentation

This is an indicator for the expliciteness of the period recorded here as the time during which the specimen was held by a previous collection.

---+++++ Content

This is an indicator for the expliciteness of the period recorded here as the time during which a specimen was held by a previous collection. 

This is set to 'True' if the specimen was held during the period indicated. It is set to false if the specimen was held by the collection at some point of time during the period, and is not filled if unknown or if no period is specified. 


---++++ Recommended or Prescribed Values

true 

false 

empty 

---+++++ Example Value

The specimen was held by the previous collection for exactly the time given as the period. 

The specimen was held by the previous collection during the period recorded here. 

The exact timing and/or duration of the period that the specimen was held by the previous collection is unknown or no period is specified.
