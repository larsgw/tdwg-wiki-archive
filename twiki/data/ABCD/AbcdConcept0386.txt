---+++++ Full Name

Start of unit identification (Julian day number)

---+++++ Documentation

The Julian day number in the year for the beginning of the unit identification event period.

---+++++ Content

Note that this element is not normally used for identifications.

The Julian day number (or sequential day) in the year for the exact date or for the beginning day of a period representing the unit identification event. The year is given in ISO-Date-Time-Begin.

The content range is 1-365 for normal years, 366 for leap years.
