---+++++ Full Name

Start of gathering event (Julian day number)

---+++++ Documentation

The Julian day number in the year for the beginning of the gathering event.

---+++++ Content

The Julian day number (or sequential day) in the year for the exact date or for the first day of the gathering event. The year is given in ISO-Date-Time-Begin. 

The content range is 1-365 for normal years, 366 for leap years. 

Maps to Darwin Core 2: Julian Day.
