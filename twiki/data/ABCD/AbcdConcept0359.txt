---+++++ Documentation

The suffix for the name of the unit identifier.

---+++++ Content

The suffix for the name of the unit identifier. The suffix should be output after the name, regardless of whether it is in sorting sequence (Inherited, Given) or not. 

Examples: 'Jun.', 'III.'. (vCard: N-Suffix)
