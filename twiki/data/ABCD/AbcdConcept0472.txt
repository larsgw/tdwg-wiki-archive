---+++++ Full Name

Person specimen was acquired from (inherited name prefix)

---+++++ Documentation

The prefix to the inherited name of the person from whom the specimen was acquired.

---+++++ Content

The prefix to the inherited name of the person from whom the specimen was acquired. The prefix should be output before the name, but is usually not included in sorting. Compare Title for 'Prof', 'Dr.' (vCard: N-Prefix)
