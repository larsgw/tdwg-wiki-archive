---+++++ Full Name

Named collection or survey including unit

---+++++ Documentation

A named collection or survey project to which the unit belongs, which may not match the <verbatim>SourceName</verbatim>.

---+++++ Content

A collection or survey project to which the unit belongs. This collection or survey may have a distinctive name that does not match the <verbatim>SourceName</verbatim>. 

Note that this element refers to the name accepted by the specimen or data holding institution and this may or may not be different from the project name given under <verbatim>Gathering</verbatim>.
