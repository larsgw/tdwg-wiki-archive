---+++++ Content

Title for the role of the person or organisation from whom the specimen was acquired. This element can be used to provide a title such as "Database Administrator" or "Curator" when no individual person is named. 

Analogue to vCard: Role : Functional contact name (e.g. "Database administrator", "The Director", etc.).
