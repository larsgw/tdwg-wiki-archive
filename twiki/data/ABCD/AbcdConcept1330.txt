---+++++ Full Name

Gathering site aspect

---+++++ Documentation

The facing direction (aspect) of the entire gathering site or a sample point.

---+++++ Content

The facing direction (aspect) of the entire gathering site or a sample point.

This is measured in either letters (e.g. NE, SW) or Degrees (e.g. 270).

This information is of particular interest in relation to exposure and incident sunlight.

The values in these fields are nearly always directional and restricted to mountainous or sloping ground.
