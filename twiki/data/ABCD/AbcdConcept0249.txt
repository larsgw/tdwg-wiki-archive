---+++++ Documentation

The suffix for the name of the administrative contact for the unit record.

---+++++ Content

The suffix for the name of the administrative contact for the unit record. The suffix should be output after the name, regardless of whether it is in sorting sequence (Inherited, Given) or not. 

Examples: 'Jun.', 'III.'. (vCard: N-Suffix)
