---+++++ Full Name

End of gathering event (Julian day number)

---+++++ Documentation

The Julian day number of the last day of the gathering event. 

---+++++ Content

The Julian day number (sequential day within the year) of the last day of the gathering event. 

The year is given in ISODateTimeEnd. 

The content range is 1-365 for normal years, 366 for leap years.
