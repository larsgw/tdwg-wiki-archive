A language-specific label representation of the organisation acting as or representing the specimen preparation agent.

---+++++ Content

A language-specific label representation of the name of the organisation acting as or representing the specimen preparation agent. The attribute @language lets you specify the language, the Representation mechanism makes it possible to ensure that only one name per language is specified.
