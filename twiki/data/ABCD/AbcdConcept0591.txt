---+++++ Full Name

Previous collection held specimen until (time of day)

---+++++ Documentation

The time of day in hours from midnight for the end of the period during which the specimen was held by the previous collection.

---+++++ Content

The time of day in decimal hours from midnight local time for the end of the period during which the specimen was held by the previous collection.

Note that times are decimal hours: 13.5 represents 1:30 pm, not 1:05 pm, nor 1:50 pm. 

---++++ Recommended or Prescribed Values


---+++++ Example Value
