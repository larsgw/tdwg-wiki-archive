---+++++ Subgroup


---+++++ Full Name
Named place relation derived flag

---+++++ Documentation
A flag indicating that the named place relationship or the placename given here is the result of an interpretation of the original data.

---+++++ Content
A flag indicating that the name of - or the relation to - the place is not that originally indicated by the collector, but has been derived (e.g. by means of transforming a historical place name to a modern one, by translating the place name, by tranforming values like "upriver" to compass bearings, etc.)

---++++ Recommended or Prescribed Values
True, False, or not stated.
