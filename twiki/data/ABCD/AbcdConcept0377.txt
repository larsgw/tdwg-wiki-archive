---+++++ Documentation

A container element for references that were used by the identifier to provide the identification.

---+++++ Content

A container element for references that were used by the identifier to provide the identification (e.g. by means of providing a key or a description of the taxon). It circumscribes the taxon identified (and thus can act as a potential taxon's sec. reference). 

Note: this is not the citation from which an identification was taken, see under Identifier/Identification-Source.
