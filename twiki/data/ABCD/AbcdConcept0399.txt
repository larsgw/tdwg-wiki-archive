---+++++ Content

The history of identifications for this unit (assigned identification results).

This is a free text field for use when extracting data from datasources where the identification or verification history (or part of it) is a free text field. 

Under normal circumstances the history can be constructed by sorting identification events by date and reference to the preferred identification flag.
