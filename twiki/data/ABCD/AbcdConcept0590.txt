---+++++ Full Name

Previous collection held specimen until (Julian day number)

---+++++ Documentation

The Julian day number for the last day that a previous collection held the specimen.

---+++++ Content

The Julian day number (sequential day within the year) of the last day that the specimen was held by a previous collection.

The year is given in ISO<verbatim>DateTimeEnd</verbatim>.

The content range is 1-365 for normal years, 366 for leap years.
