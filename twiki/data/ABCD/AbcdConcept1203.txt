A container element for several elements expressing stratigraphy at the gathering site.

---+++++ Content

A container element for several elements expressing stratigraphy at the gathering site (geological dating and rock unit names related to the site or sample locations).

The structure allows for a full analysis of stratigraphy (e.g. to allow fossils in collections to be sorted by period and zone. Also allows for free text terms when their status is unknown or for more full descriptions of the stratigraphy of site).
