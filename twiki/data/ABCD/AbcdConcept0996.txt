---+++++ Full Name
UTM Northing

---+++++ Documentation
The distance in meters north of the origin for the UTMSubzone. 

---+++++ Content
The distance in meters north of the origin for the UTMSubzone. For the northern hemisphere (UTMSubzones M through X) the origin is at the equator. For the southern hemisphere (Subzones C through L), the origin is at 10000 meters south of the equator. 

(values constrained to be integers between 0 and 10000)


---++++ Recommended or Prescribed Values
integers between 0 and 10000
