---+++++ Content

The altitude of the gathering site land or floor level, given as distance above (positive) or below mean sea level. (Note the difference from the <verbatim>Height</verbatim> and <verbatim>Depth</verbatim> elements, which are measured relative to a local surface.)

Although the altitude could be covered by the generic element for site measurements, the separate altitude element is preserved for the sake of clarity.
