---+++++ Content

A cultivar name identified for the unit, as specified by the ICNCP. 

For output, cultivar names must be enclosed in single quotes.

Either a registered or other formal designation given to the accession. First letter uppercase. Multiple names separated with semicolon without space. A FAO/IPGRI multi-crop passport descriptor (MCPD), description and examples taken from EURISCO descriptor list dated July 3, 2002.
