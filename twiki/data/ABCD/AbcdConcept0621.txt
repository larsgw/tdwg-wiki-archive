Author team of the basionym of the nomenclatural type designation for the specimen.

---+++++ Content

Author team of the basionym of a combination that is the nomenclatural type designation for the specimen.

Author(s) of the original name for a taxon which has subsequently been revised but retains the same stem or epithet. May be preceded by a sanctioning author. If possible, preceding "ex" authors should be avoided.
