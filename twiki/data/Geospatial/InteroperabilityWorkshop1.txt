---++ TDWG TIP Workshop

A workshop funded under the TDWG TIP programme was held in Campinas, Brazil in the first week of April. The aim of the workshop was to explore interoperability between TDWG related and geospatial related services. This page provides the resources related to this workshop.

To check the final prototype we created go to the  [[http://omtest.cria.org.br/biogeosdi/][Testbed Web Site]] or check the [[%ATTACHURL%/BioGeoSDIreport.pdf][Final Report in pdf]].

---+++ Final Outcomes

This part is still in progress as we wrap things up. The prototype and files are in a temporary machine at CRIA. This machine is very slow and can be unreachable. Please be patient while we move everything to a TDWG server.
