Because of the great similarities between the Web Feature Service of the OGC community and TAPIR, in this document we describe the differences. Someone comming from the OGC community will easily understand TAPIR as a comparison to WFS and TAPIR implementations can use this document to consider implementing WFS too.

This is a highly informal document and may be unstructured.
