*Question:* How do you change links and information on the page http://www.tdwg.org/activities/? 

*Answer:* This is a tricky one but here are the instructions:

   1. Log on to the Typo3 backend (http://www.tdwg.org/typo3)
   1. Click the "List" menu on the left-hand side
   1. Click the item "Front-end Users" on the website tree (after the Homepage and before TDWG2006)
   1. Scroll the contents panel all the way down to the "Search String" field, type Literature and click "Search"
   1. Click on the pencil icon near the item "Literature Interest Group" under the list of Website Usergroups.
   1. Click Edit in the pop-up
   1. Change the Wiki field accordingly.
   1. Click the "save" button.
