---+!! Welcome to the %WEB% wiki

Welcome to the home of %WEB%. This web-based collaboration area (wiki) is used for discussions of the %WEB% Interest Group of the [[http://www.tdwg.org][Taxonomic Databases Working Group (TDWG)]].

The [[http://www.tdwg.org/activities/tnc/][Taxonomic Concepts and Names Subgroup]] page on the TDWG site.

The [[http://lists.tdwg.org/mailman/listinfo/tdwg-tnc][TNC mailing list]] web page.

The [[http://www.tdwg.org/activities/tnc/charter/][Charter]] for the TNC interest group on the TDWG site.

The [[http://www.tdwg.gbif.org/uploads/media/v101.xsd][Current Version]] of the TCS standard on the TDWG site.

An  [[http://tdwg.napier.ac.uk][archive of early developments and discussions of TCS]]

---+News
   *  New TNC task group being set up - TCS Usability. Please see below and get involved if it's relevant to you.

   * Development work on the TCS has been temporarily diverted to work on the [[http://wiki.tdwg.org/twiki/bin/view/TAG/TDWGOntology][TDWGOntology]] where we are using the TCS as a basis for defining a modular ontology for TDWG in collaboration with members of the [[http://www.tdwg.gbif.org/subgroups/abcd/][ABCD]], [[http://www.tdwg.gbif.org/subgroups/sdd/][SDD]] and [[http://www.tdwg.gbif.org/subgroups/darwincore/][Darwin Core]] subgroups of TDWG. 

    * As a result of the Ontology work TCS will be available in RDF format and OWL format.
