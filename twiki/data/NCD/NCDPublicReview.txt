---++NCD Public Review
All TDWG standards undergo a process of review (see http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/process/). NCD's public review provides for anyone to comment on the proposed standard. All comments should be made at http://www.tdwg.org/stdtrack/article/view/312 using *Add comment* on the right hand side. If you wish to upload a document, please use the Attach function at the bottom of this page and *then* add your comment at the above URL and include a link in the comment to this page.

*Please do not add comments to this page!*

Lee Belbin
TDWG Secretariat
