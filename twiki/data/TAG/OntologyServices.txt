   * Wiki based ontology editor
   * Vocabulary repository

      * Meaning and usage
      * Relationships

   *  Internationalization
   * Terms: one per page
   * Graph of pages = Graph of terms

      * Privileges

      * Site
      * Branch

      * RDF,
      * OWL,
      * SKOS
