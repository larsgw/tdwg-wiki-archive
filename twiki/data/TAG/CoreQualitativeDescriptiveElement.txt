---++Discussion

After much discussion, SDD has decided to use the term Categorical in preference for Qualitative. One major reason for this is that the term "Qualitative" (i.e. an unmeasurable essence of a thing) is inappropriate where continuous measurements are classified into categories.

Please rename this element to CategoricalData, following the SDD standard
