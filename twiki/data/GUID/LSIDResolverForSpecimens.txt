---++ LSID Resolver for Specimens

*Coordinator(s):* Steve Perry.

*Participants:* Steve Perry, Robert Gales, Dave Vieglais

----
---+++ Description
This group will develop a prototype LSID resolver for specimens. The LSID resolver will be part of the LSID authority system integrated into DiGIR2 Server (a general purpose RDF metadata server).  DiGIR2 is being developed at the University of Kansas by Dave Vieglais, Steve Perry, and Robert Gales.
