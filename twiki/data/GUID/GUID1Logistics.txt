---++++ Transportation to the hotel

*Airport Transportation:* When you arrive at the Raleigh/Durham International airport, please *contact the hotel from the courtesy telephone in the luggage claim area* when you are ready for a driver to meet you.  Transportation is provided for $30.00 per person each way. Payment for the shuttle service is settled upon your arrival and registration at the hotel. Children travel complimentary with an adult.

You will be staying at the [[http://www.millenniumhotels.com/MCIL.nsf/lu_hoteldoc/113$$hoteldescription?opendocument][Millenium Hotel Durham]]. The address is:

*2800 Campus Walk Avenue, Durham, NC, USA 27705-4479*
*tel: +1 919 383 8575, 800.633.5379 toll free, fax: +1 919 383 8495, email: durham@mhrmail.com*

*Directions to the hotel:*
   1.  From Raleigh Durham Airport access I40 West
   1.  From I40 West exit off onto 147 Durham Freeway
   1.  Take exit 15/501 BYPASS South exit off of the Freeway
   1.  From 15/501 BYPASS South take the Morreene Road Exit, make a left at the top of the ramp, and another left onto Campus Walk Avenue at the first traffic light, hotel will be on your left.  

*Local Transportation:* is provided to Duke Campus, Medical Center and local restaurants.  

*Hotel Location:*  The 313-room Millennium Hotel Durham is located only minutes from Duke University and Medical Center and is also near the University of North Carolina Chapel Hill, North Carolina Central University, downtown Durham and Research Triangle Park. Raleigh/Durham International Airport is 17 miles, 20 minutes drive time, from the Millennium Hotel Durham.
