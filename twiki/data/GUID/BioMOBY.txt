---+++ BioMOBY
From BioMOBY web site ([[http://www.biomoby.org]]): 

   "BioMOBY is an international research project involving biological data hosts, biological data service providers, and coders whose aim is to explore various methodologies for biological data representation, distribution, and discovery."

See [[http://wiki.gbif.org/dadiwiki/wikka.php?wakka=GBIFAndMOBY][GBIF And MOBY]] (Markus Döring et al.) for a comparison of BioMOBY with TDWG/GBIF data sharing using DiGIR and related protocols.
