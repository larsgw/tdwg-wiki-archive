---+++TDWG Accomplishments that Should Be Promoted
   1. New [[http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/constitution/][constitution]] and [[http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/process/][processes (what it implies)]]
   1. New [[http://www.tdwg.org/activities/][Interest and Task Groups]]
   1. New [[http://www.tdwg.org][Web site]], [[http://wiki.tdwg.org/][Wiki]], [[http://www.tdwg.org/ojs/][OJS]], [[http://www.tdwg.org/blog/][blog]], [[http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/news/][News]], [[http://www.tdwg.org/standards/][standards repository]] and [[http://lists.tdwg.org/mailman/listinfo][mail list manager]]. It would be particularly good to promote the blog as a mechanism of presenting new (provocative) ideas.
   1. New [[http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/faq/][FAQ]], [[http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/glossary/][Glossary]] and [[http://www.tdwg.org/activities/general-tasks/][Help for online systems]]
   1. [[http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/][TDWG brochure and poster]]
   1. A number of [[http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/][PowerPoint presentations]] about TDWG suitable for use at other meetings
   1. Site [[http://www.tdwg.org/membership/][registration and membership (with online payments) and *donations*]]
   1. The [[http://www.tdwg.org/proceedings/issue/current][Proceedings of TDWG]]. *We must promote this!*
   1. [[http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/news/article/tdwg-infrastructure-project/][Round 2 projects and contracts]]
   1. [[http://www.tdwg.org/activities/guid/][LSID developments and directions]]. We now have IPNI, Index Fungorum, ZooBank and soon Species 2000 deploying LSIDs.
   1. [[http://www.tdwg.org/activities/tapir/][TAPIR/TAPIRLink developments and directions]] (including BigDig)
   1. Interest and Task Group meetings?
   1. Geointeroperability demo (being loaded to the TDWG site soon I hope!)
   1. [[http://www.tdwg.org/conference2007/][TDWG 2007 in Bratislava]]
   1. [[http://www.tdwg.org/about-tdwg/][New agreements e.g., OGC MoU]]
   1. Implementations of TDWG standards/protocols
   1. Executive Summaries of TDWG standards
   1. Video clips about TAPIR and LSIDs ready for You Tube etc
   1. Current activities
   1. Meeting reports
   1. The database of > 500 [[http://www.tdwg.org/biodiv-projects/[international biodiversity data projects]]
   1. What else?
