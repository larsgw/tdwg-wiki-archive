---+ Australasian Species Profile Models

This page provides a link to discussions in Australia through [[http://www.chah.gov.au][Council of Heads of Australasian Herbaria]]  (CHAH) [[http://hiscom.chah.org.au/wiki/Main_Page][Herbarium Information Systems Committee]] (HISCOM) to integrate a number of existing electronic Flora projects, each with individual schemas and content design.

Project schemas can be found from these links:
