---+Article 9: Dissolution

Dissolution of TDWG can only be enacted by a two-thirds majority of the members and by a two-thirds of the institutional members by vote. Notification of dissolution must be dispatched by the Executive Committee to the membership at least ninety days before close of voting.
 
Upon dissolution of TDWG, any net remaining assets shall be transferred to the International Association for Plant Taxonomy or its successor.
