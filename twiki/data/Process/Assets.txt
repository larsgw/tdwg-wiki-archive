---+Article 7:  Assets

The revenue of TDWG shall consist of membership dues, of income from and sales of publications and lease of rights, of grants, donations and legacies, of income from investments and of all other kinds of income that are compatible with the aims of TDWG. 

The assets of TDWG shall be used exclusively for the statutory aims and purposes, and in no case to the immediate profit of its members. 

The financial year shall be the calendar year.
